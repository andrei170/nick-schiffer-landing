FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

I already follow @nsbuilders. Is this just the same content?

No. The content shows the work - what the build looks like, how decisions get made on site. The Playbook installs the system behind the decisions: the estimating sheet, the sub retention framework, the weekly cadence, the intake process. The content is the why. The Playbook is the how, made installable.

How fast will I see results?

Most builders install the estimating module (module 2) within the first 7 days of enrolment and catch at least one margin error in the next bid they write. The sub retention conversation takes longer - usually one good hire cycle. The weekly cadence typically changes Fridays within 4-6 weeks of module 3 going live.

Is this the Modern Craftsman Mastermind?

No. The Mastermind is the high-ticket group cohort for $1M+ custom builders and is separate. The Custom Builder Mentorship is the hands-on program that sits below it. Many mentorship alumni apply for the Mastermind 6-12 months later once the systems from the mentorship are running.

What is the time commitment?

3-4 hours per week for 12 weeks. Module video is async. One live Q&A with Nick the first Tuesday of every month. Recordings available if you miss the live session.

What is the investment?

The Playbook is available at two tiers. The standard tier includes the full curriculum, monthly Q&A, and lifetime access. The premium tier adds a personal video review of your custom build business by Nick. Both tiers are a one-time payment.

What if I am past $5M in custom residential?

The Playbook is built for $1M-$5M operators. If you are past $5M, modules 5 and 6 are still relevant - but the on-ramp problems we solve in modules 1-4 are mostly behind you. The Modern Craftsman Mastermind application is the better fit.

Refund policy?

Yes. Complete the first two modules, decide the system is not a fit for your business, full refund within 14 days of enrolment. No questions.

Will the templates work in my market?

Yes. The templates are build-stage-specific, not market-specific. Eastern MA, Pacific Northwest, Texas, anywhere - if you are $1M-$5M custom residential, the operations systems install.
